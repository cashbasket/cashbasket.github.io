About Ryan

Ryan Machan has been into coding for a long time. From the day he first learned a few HTML tags from a high school classmate, Ryan has been consistently working on websites and honing his craft, both for his own enjoyment and [hopefully] the enjoyment of others.

Ryan has worked primarily in the higher education sector, creating sophisticated websites and web applications for use by faculty, staff and students. His passion is learning new programming languages, frameworks, and concepts in general. While he can't learn them all, he can certainly try.

After becoming proficient with frontend design and development, Ryan was introduced to the backend via Coldfusion and Microsoft SQL Server. After that, he learned all about object-oriented programming via C#. These days, he primarily builds sites using ASP.NET (C#) and JavaScript (Node.js) with SQL Server, MySQL, and even Firebase; while backend JavaScript is technically his most recent major discovery, it is probably his most pleasant discovery as well.

In his spare time, Ryan is an avid music listener (and was even a highly competent musician in a past life); he has probably spent more money on music than groceries at this point. He also enjoys horror movies, football, sci-fi anything, video games of all kinds, and discovering utterly baffling memes.
